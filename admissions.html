Requirements

What You Need to Apply

Requirements vary by program track. English-track programs require proof of English proficiency (TOEFL, IELTS, or equivalent). All applicants must provide official academic transcripts.

English Track — Undergraduate

High school diploma or equivalent, English proficiency certificate, personal statement, and two letters of recommendation.

English Track — Graduate

Bachelor's degree (or equivalent), English proficiency, research proposal or statement of purpose, and academic transcripts.

Korean Language Program

Open to international students of any level. No prior Korean knowledge required for beginner classes. Short-term and semester-long options available.
