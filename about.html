About Sejong

A Legacy of Creative Excellence Since 1940

Founded in 1940, Sejong University is a leading private research university in the heart of Seoul, committed to fostering global citizens and innovative thinkers.

Vision

A University That Creates a Brighter World

Sejong University's vision is to become a globally recognized creative university that cultivates talented individuals capable of leading the future. Rooted in Daeyang (Great Ocean) spirit, we embrace openness, challenge, and service.

Founded

Established in 1940, Sejong University has grown into one of Korea's leading private universities with a heritage spanning over eight decades.

Location

Our main campus is located at 209 Neungdong-ro, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul — well-connected to the city and close to major cultural and business districts.

Global Reach

Sejong maintains partnerships with over 84 universities worldwide and actively recruits international students through English-track programs and exchange agreements.

By the numbers

Sejong at a Glance

From research institutes to partner universities, Sejong's impact reaches across Korea and around the world.

10 Undergraduate Colleges

Liberal Arts, Social Sciences, Business & Economics, Hospitality & Tourism, Natural Sciences, Life Science, AI Convergence, Engineering, Arts & Physical Education, and Daeyang College of Humanities.

7 Graduate Schools

Graduate School, Business, Public Policy, Education, Tourism, Interdisciplinary Arts, and Industry — offering masters and doctoral programs across disciplines.

Top Government Certification

Sejong received the highest grade in government certification assessing universities' internationalization capabilities — recognising excellence in global education.
