Research highlights

Recent Breakthroughs

Networks

Satellite Network Optimisation

A new deployment model that reduces complexity of large-scale satellite networks while maintaining continuous global coverage.

Energy

Faster-Charging Battery Technology

Development of a next-generation silicon anode enabling faster charging and longer battery life for electric vehicles and energy storage systems.

AI

Neuromorphic On-Device AI

Technology performing AI computations directly on edge devices — eliminating reliance on cloud servers for real-time intelligence.

Security

Acoustic Signal Air-Gap Attacks

Research addressing modern cybersecurity vulnerabilities in air-gapped systems through acoustic signal-based threat analysis.

Climate

Heatwave & Drought in Eurasia

Analysis of anthropogenic greenhouse gas effects on global temperature shifts and their regional impact on South Korea's climate.

LLM

Scalable Transformer Accelerator

Hardware technology enabling on-device execution of large language models like BERT and GPT without external computation infrastructure.
